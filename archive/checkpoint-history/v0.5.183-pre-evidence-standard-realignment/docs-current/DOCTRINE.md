# Canonical doctrine

## Goal

Build a broad, accurate, maintainable learner-facing parser for Cantonese as independently documented and naturally used. Linguistic accuracy and learner safety outrank parser neatness, legacy continuity, fixture stability, implementation convenience, and source prestige.

## Claim classes

Every consequential parser statement must be classified as one of:

1. **Cantonese language claim** — form, meaning, distribution, ambiguity, restriction, or productivity.
2. **Lexicalized-language claim** — a bounded conventional expression without a productivity claim.
3. **Parser representation** — an internal grouping or node name.
4. **Parser heuristic** — a defeasible operational rule.
5. **Evidence gap, quarantine, or retirement record**.

Language and lexicalized-language claims require external authority. Internal representations and heuristics require design provenance, explicit non-licensing safeguards, and tests; they are not linguistic evidence.

## Authority-origin rule

A new or promoted language claim must begin with screened external propositions, not parser behavior. Record exact locators, the proposition actually supported, the relationship to the parser claim, limitations, and forbidden inferences.

Code, parser output, fixtures, generated probes, regression success, learner usefulness, and corpus candidate retrieval cannot bootstrap a language claim. They may test a source-originated design or identify a neutral research question.

## Evidence-scope rule

- Attestation proves that a form occurred in the documented context; it does not by itself prove productivity, frequency, dialect-wide naturalness, or the parser's preferred structure.
- A narrow supported subtype does not promote a broad compatibility label.
- Source quantity cannot compensate for dependence, weak locators, copied examples, or mismatched scope.
- Pedagogical sources may establish explicit teaching analyses and examples, but should not alone establish unrestricted productivity.
- Internal corpus material can expose natural usage and parser failures, but cannot by itself promote grammar.

## Native-conflict escalation

When published analysis conflicts with recorded native-speaker naturalness, publication attestation proves only that the form was printed, elicited, or analyzed. The exact disputed family freezes and becomes non-promotable until the conflict is independently resolved through evidence appropriate to the dispute, including:

- genuinely independent source diversity;
- controlled minimal contrasts;
- explicit regional, register, lexical, speaker, and discourse investigation where relevant;
- multiple speakers with useful metadata;
- negative boundaries and competing analyses;
- a fresh prospective held-out packet;
- no unresolved conflict on the exact promoted family.

Do not invent dialect, register, or contextual explanations. A native judgment evaluates surface naturalness within its elicitation conditions; it does not by itself validate the parser's internal analysis.

## Productive promotion

A `supported_productive` construction must be narrow, source-originated, and pass all required evidence and evaluation gates. The default minimum includes at least three independent natural attestations of the exact family, unless a work package documents and justifies a stronger alternative evidence design. Attestations are necessary but not sufficient: controlled variation, negative boundaries, competing analyses, prospective held-out success, and learner-facing render review remain mandatory.

## Productive-program completion rule

The project may use universal resolution as an operational target, but it must not equate every runtime label with a productive Cantonese construction. Every retained public language construction should ultimately be either `supported_productive` or an explicitly bounded nonproductive claim. Internal wrappers, diagnostics, heuristics, compatibility aliases, duplicates, and misanalyses must instead become non-licensing, merge, decompose, retire, or remain quarantined.

A falling count of public labels can therefore represent progress when broad or false claims are replaced by narrower supported constructions. Status accounting must report productive subtypes separately from lexicalized, internal, retired, and quarantined outcomes.

## Parser integrity

- Never insert hidden subjects, objects, nouns, resources, propositions, results, activities, or connectives.
- Preserve ambiguity when evidence does not determine a unique analysis.
- Internal wrappers cannot independently license Cantonese behavior.
- Compatibility aliases cannot broaden an accepted internal subtype.
- Learner-facing simplification must remain materially true.
- Regression results are change signals, not linguistic authority.
- Vocabulary and Jyutping additions do not count as grammar evidence.

## Autonomous governance

The assistant may autonomously research, define packets, freeze or unfreeze bounded work, implement, validate, accept, revise, reject, quarantine, retire, and package changes under this doctrine. Separate user approval is not required for each parser family.

Every work package must still define exact scope, source propositions, positive and negative cases, ambiguous cases, visible render cases, permitted and forbidden changes, fresh sealed prospective held-out commitments, and acceptance or rollback criteria. Authority for one package never silently expands to another family.
