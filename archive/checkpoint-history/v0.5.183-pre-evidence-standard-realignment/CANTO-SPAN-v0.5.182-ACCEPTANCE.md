# Canto Span v0.5.182 acceptance

v0.5.182 is accepted.

## Scope

The accepted productive construction is internal `ResourceUseLaiFunctionRelation`, publicly displayed through compatibility label `IntendedFunctionRelation`, for an overt non-person resource, artifact, or spatial part followed directly by `用嚟` or `用來` and an overt non-perfective action predicate stating the resource's assigned or intended function.

The initial resource remains neutral between subject and topic. The parser inserts no hidden resource, noun, subject, object, purpose, or activity.

## Evidence and review

- three independent natural attestations:
  - Wong 2023: `呢個用嚟切嘢`;
  - Hong Kong Memory interview-derived narrative: `另一邊用來儲物`;
  - Countryside Conservation Office direct homeowner quotation: `呢間屋用嚟擺嘢、養豬養牛`;
- Cheung 2018 as independent structural support for purposive `嚟` and conventionalized `用來/用嚟`;
- Chor 2018 and Matthews 2006 as boundary and competing-analysis evidence only;
- 15 rendered rows and 133/133 render assertions PASS;
- eight fresh prospective held-out commitments verified and eight evaluations PASS;
- inherited regression, source accounting, provenance, native-conflict, naturalness, topology, semantic, corpus, and documentation gates PASS.

Generated probes, render examples, fixtures, corpus candidate hits, and held-out cases were not counted as independent natural attestations.

## Boundaries

The promotion excludes:

- a separate overt user;
- modal affordance and copular frames;
- perfective or actual-use events;
- ordinary instrumental `用`;
- general purposive `嚟` without the direct `用嚟/用來` profile;
- negation, A-not-A, and reduplication;
- missing resources or function predicates;
- person-like resources;
- hidden argument insertion.

Broad `IntendedFunctionRelation` remains provisional.

## Integrity

The exact parser candidate was frozen before evaluator custody was opened. All eight evaluator cases were opened once, passed, and are consumed. The later evidence closure changes legitimacy metadata only; parser behavior remains the frozen behavior that passed render and held-out evaluation.

The global grammar freeze remains active for every other unaccepted claim. `supported_productive` is now 2: `PostverbalZoPerfectiveVP` and `ResourceUseLaiFunctionRelation`.
