## Current authorization

Work autonomously to maximize the quality of the **research-based acceptance audit** of grammar already represented by Canto Span.

The current authorization includes:

- linguistic research;
- corpus discovery and manual adjudication;
- source dossiers;
- held-out and boundary-set preparation;
- competing-analysis review;
- native/expert screening design;
- parser-neutral probes;
- legitimacy documentation;
- frozen remediation records;
- workflow, validation, and documentation improvements;
- verified recovery packaging.
- source-first discovery for future grammar, provided the question is neutral and implementation is not begun;
- quarantine, downgrade, lexicalization, internal-representation conversion, and retirement of source-less legacy claims.

The current authorization excludes:

- new grammar implementation before authority-origin synthesis and separate batch authorization;
- research framed to confirm a parser label, hierarchy, or preferred answer;
- registry promotion;
- unsupported legitimacy promotion;
- treating regression preservation as a linguistic requirement;
- cosmetic learner-role-only work without material learner harm;
- A2 work.

Replacement is permitted when evidence warrants it but is not a precondition for genuine source-origin coverage. A materially false learner explanation may receive a standalone correction when waiting would preserve learner harm; role evidence still cannot establish grammar.

5. Natural data provenance.

## Autonomous stopping conditions

Stop the affected branch when progress materially requires unavailable rendered Obsidian evidence, inaccessible material sources, native/expert judgment, separate authorization to change runtime grammar, or creation/verification of recovery artifacts fails. Export an in-progress recovery checkpoint before stopping.

Do not stop merely because evidence disagrees. Preserve the disagreement and choose `provisional`, `research_pending`, or `defer` when warranted.
