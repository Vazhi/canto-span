Historical text remains auditable but is not active instruction.

## Goal

Build a broad, accurate, maintainable learner-facing parser for Cantonese as independently documented and naturally used. Parser neatness, label continuity, fixture stability, and source prestige are subordinate to that goal.

## Provenance rule

Every consequential statement has a provenance type:

- Cantonese form, meaning, distribution, restriction, productivity, ambiguity, or linguistic analysis requires an explicit edge to independently accountable external evidence with an exact locator and limitation.
- A corpus record establishes only the attested material and context actually preserved.
- Parser behavior, diagnostics, code paths, tests, and engineering decisions use exact internal provenance and cannot establish Cantonese grammar.
- Internal behavior may open a neutral research question, never a hypothesis, expected answer, or favored replacement.
- Source disagreement is data to preserve, not noise to select away.

## Language-claim lifecycle

No language behavior advances out of order:

```text
neutral observable question
→ preregistered discovery protocol
→ complete serious-candidate source screen
→ atomic source propositions with exact locators
→ contradiction, boundary, access, and independence record
→ narrow source-derived synthesis
→ explicit claim–source edges
→ immutable design and held-out evaluation
→ separate implementation authorization
→ semantic, boundary, corpus, and regression adjudication
→ rendered review when learner output changes
→ explicit acceptance
```

The source screen is not a vote. Authoritative sources can be incomplete or theory-dependent. No source's node names or syntactic theory automatically become Canto Span's representation.

## Legacy claims

- A legacy language claim with no explicit pattern-specific edge is non-promotable and must be downgraded, quarantined, lexicalized, converted to an internal representation statement, or retired.
- A compatible source found after an internal claim is `legacy_source_linked_posthoc`, never authority-originated evidence.
- Accepted fixtures and aggregate PASS preserve behavior only; they do not repair an origin defect.
- External evidence may justify deliberate retirement or rebaselining of accepted behavior.

## Anti-bias safeguards

- Freeze the question and search design before answer-seeking.
- Search English and Chinese terms, orthographic variants, source-introduced terminology, counterexamples, optionality, ambiguity, non-targets, and competing analyses.
- Log every serious inclusion, exclusion, duplicate, and access failure.
- Preserve complete lexical candidate sets before semantic adjudication where feasible.
- Do not infer ungrammaticality from corpus non-recovery.
- Do not double-count overlapping authors, datasets, dissertations/articles, or mirrors.
- Do not tune a rule on examples later described as held out.
- Treat source scope, period, register, speaker variation, and data sparsity as binding limitations.

## Evaluation and implementation

- Freeze positive, negative, ambiguous, boundary, and held-out rows before implementation.
- Parser probes may diagnose current behavior during research; grammar edits remain separate.
- A regression is a change signal. It is not evidence that the old or new output is linguistically correct.
- No hidden subject, object, proposition, result, activity, connective, or semantic token may be inserted.
- Learner display may simplify internal structure only when the mapping is transparent and not materially false.
- Headless validation cannot substitute for rendered Obsidian review of learner-facing changes.

## Native and expert evidence

Record explicit `natural_in_context`, `unnatural_in_context`, `unsure_or_needs_context`, or `not_seen`. A blank response is missing evidence. Naturalness can support a surface judgment within task context; it cannot validate parser hierarchy or productivity by itself.

## Recovery and interruption safety

No meaningful evidence, conclusion, decision, test result, or unresolved blocker may exist only in chat. Export a verified, downloadable recovery artifact at:

1. question freeze;
2. source-screen freeze;
3. proposition-extraction completion or material change;
4. synthesis/disposition;
5. pre-implementation;
6. material interruption risk or user request.

If a required artifact cannot be created and verified, stop substantive work. A new project/chat should import the curated clean-continuation bundle, not historical conversation memory.
