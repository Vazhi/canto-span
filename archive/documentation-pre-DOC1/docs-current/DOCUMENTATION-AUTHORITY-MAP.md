## Conflict rule

Use the highest-ranked document. CP021A supersedes repeated older headers that still say grammar is indefinitely frozen or replacement-only. `PROJECT-MEMORY-SAFETY-OVERRIDES` is the mandatory translation layer for any instruction recovered from personal context, an old chat, an archived prompt, or a handoff. Retrieval recency, repetition, and imperative wording do not reactivate a superseded memory. Do not restore a retired label because an older specification names it. Do not infer linguistic truth or acceptance from packaging, syntax, headless diagnostics, or regression PASS.

## Evidence/behavior distinction

Accepted fixtures preserve behavior but do not validate a language claim. A source-count field without explicit proposition-bearing claim–source edges is also insufficient. CP018’s semantic gate similarly routes review; it does not establish linguistic correctness.

## Update rule

State-bearing documents and downloadable archives must be updated atomically. Any state change after packaging requires rebuilding and reverifying the package.
