# `V完咗O` evidence reassessment — CP022-P1

Date: 2026-07-19  
Scope: research wording and disposition only; **no runtime behavior changed**.

## Question

Can Canto Span support a claim about Cantonese `V完咗O` despite the project native reviewer rejecting `食完咗飯` and `我食完咗飯`?

## Evidence that survives

Three published records attest or explicitly permit selected surfaces with inner `完`, outer perfective `咗`, and an overt object:

1. Sio and Bond (2025), example (2c): `我食完(咗)麵`; the paper states the order `V-IAP-OAP`.
2. Sybesma (2013), example (25a): `佢食完咗啲飯`.
3. Fan and Chan (2022), example (3c): `琴日佢做完(咗)功課喇`.

This is evidence that the sequence is present in published Cantonese descriptions. It is not frequency evidence, a multi-speaker judgment study, or proof of unrestricted productivity.

## Native evidence that must constrain the claim

The project reviewer is the user's wife, a native Cantonese speaker from Guangzhou. Her recorded judgments are:

- `食完咗飯。` — marked unnatural.
- `我食完咗飯。` — marked unnatural.
- `我食完飯。` — accepted.
- `我食完飯喇。` — accepted.

The overt-subject follow-up shows that subject omission was not the relevant problem. The contrast specifically implicates the `完咗` sequence in these tested surfaces, or its dialect/register/discourse compatibility.

## Correct disposition

`V完咗O` is:

- **published-attested for selected examples**;
- **not established as broadly natural or productive**;
- **in conflict with the sole Guangzhou-native reviewer**;
- **not eligible for `supported_productive`**;
- **not part of PFV01**, whose promoted target is only simple `V-咗-O`.

Do not infer a Hong Kong–Guangzhou dialect split from one reviewer and three published examples. Record dialect/register variation as a research question, not a conclusion.
None of the possible regional, lexical, object-form, register, or discourse explanations may be adopted without targeted evidence.

## Parser consequence

Preserve `V完咗O` only as a quarantined, source-linked subtype candidate. It must not be used as a positive learner default, a universal completion template, or evidence for the broad `CompletionVP`/`PerfectiveVP` umbrellas.

## Higher burden now governing this family

Published attestation alone is insufficient because the exact family conflicts with direct native naturalness data. Future reconsideration requires all of the following before productive retention:

1. additional independent sources that discuss distribution or variation, not merely repeat an example;
2. controlled contrasts for `V完O`, `V咗O`, `V完咗O`, object form, final particles, and discourse context;
3. multiple speakers with regional and register metadata;
4. explicit negative boundaries and competing analyses;
5. prospective held-out evaluation designed after the conflict questions are frozen.

Until then, `V完咗O` is published-attested, native-conflicted, variation-unresolved, quarantined, and outside all positive PFV01 evidence counts.
