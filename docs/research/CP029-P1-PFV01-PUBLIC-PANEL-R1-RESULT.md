## Comparison items

The comparisons were designed to separate structures, not to prove every non-target string ungrammatical.

- `我食咗。`: 5/10; objectless perfective is context-sensitive and belongs to broader `PerfectiveVP`.
- `我食完咗飯。`: 0/10; all respondents rejected it in isolation, commonly preferring either `我食完飯` or `我食咗飯`. Publication attestation remains boundary evidence only because project-native judgments conflict.
