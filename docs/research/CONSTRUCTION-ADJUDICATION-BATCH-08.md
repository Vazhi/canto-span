## AA26 — WanMarkedCompletionVP

The current `CompletionVP` suite is not one exact profile. It includes bare `V完`, `V完O`, `V完咗O`, negated `唔V完`, final-`未` questions, clause-linking contexts, and `V晒`. The checked sources support 完 as a verbal completion or phase particle and separately distinguish outer aspect 咗. They do not authorize transferring that evidence to 晒.

The UUID is retained for the inner `V + 完` completion predicate. Negation, outer perfective marking, final-`未` questions, and larger clause relations may contain that constituent but remain independently represented. `V完咗O` also remains subject to the project's higher-burden native-conflict rule; publication attestation does not settle uniform naturalness.

## AA34 — PreverbalCoverbNPClause

`CoverbFrame` corresponds to a real source-recognized structural family, but the legacy name does not expose the implemented order. The current positives instantiate an overt preverbal coverb and NP before a VP, principally locative `喺 + NP` and participant-marking `同 + NP`.

The permanent name is therefore narrowed to observable form. It does not assign one semantic relation to all coverbs, treat conjunction `同` as a coverb, license postverbal result locations, or generalize to every coverb lexeme and ordering. Marker-specific meaning remains independently constrained.

## AA37 — PostverbalDiMannerAdjustmentVP

The directly supported core is postverbal `V + property/manner + 啲`, as in `行快啲`, `講慢啲`, and `講大聲啲`. The same runtime label also appears on standalone or elliptical `快啲` and `客氣啲`, which are not adverbials modifying an overt verb.

The UUID is retained for the postverbal construction. Standalone scalar directives and discourse-separated property units require separate adjudication and cannot inherit the postverbal evidence. The already adjudicated preposed `AA38 PreposedDiMannerVPWrapper` remains an internal compositional wrapper rather than a sibling word-order variant licensed by this UUID.

## AA40 — HouMarkedPropertyPredicate

The current suite contains a strong repeated core of copula-less `好 + property predicate`, including `今日好凍`, `佢好高`, and embedded `我覺得佢好開心`. The sources support degree-modified predicative properties and independently segment 好 as a degree word.

The runtime label is nevertheless broader: it also appears on `幾耐`, `幾多`, `幾多年前`, and nominal material such as `好大壓力`. These do not all instantiate the same stative-predicate construction. The permanent identity is narrowed to overt 好 + independently licensed property predication; wh-quantity, duration, nominal-modifier, and other degree-marker profiles require separate homes.

## AA45 — SoengVPComplementClause

Every current positive uses lexical `想` with a visible VP complement, normally after an overt subject. The sources explicitly distinguish `想` and `要` rather than licensing one interchangeable desiderative category, and the executable boundary already excludes obligation-oriented `要 + VP`.

The UUID is retained under a lexeme-specific structural name. It does not include `想 + NP`, `要 + VP`, `諗住 + VP`, or preference predicates such as `鍾意`. Topic and coverb material surrounding the clause is outside the permanent identity's core.
