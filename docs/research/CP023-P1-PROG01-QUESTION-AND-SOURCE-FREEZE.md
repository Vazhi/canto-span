# CP023-P1-PROG01 question and source freeze

Date: 2026-07-20  
Status: **research source freeze complete; implementation not authorized**  
Accepted runtime: **v0.5.182**, unchanged  
Selected research candidate: **`PostverbalGanProgressiveVP`**  
Public compatibility family: **`ProgressiveVP`**

## Bounded question

Can Canto Span justify a narrow internal subtype for an overt predicate followed immediately by postverbal `緊` and an overt non-interrogative object, while keeping broad progressive syntax, lexical compatibility, `喺度`, `住`, objectless `V緊`, wh objects, directionals, modal/future environments, and `有 + V緊` outside the promoted claim?

The target is a VP subspan, so an independently licensed subject, time phrase, clause linker, or sentence-final polar particle may occur outside the target. The target inserts no subject, time, reference point, object, path, or answer.

## Frozen source propositions

1. **Postverbal `緊` is a progressive aspect marker.** It presents an event or situation as in progress at a reference time; it is not present tense and does not supply an unspoken `而家`.
2. **Overt `V緊O` is directly attested.** Published examples include `睇緊電視`, `唱緊首兒歌`, `食緊飯`, `着緊好靚嘅衫`, `買緊樓`, and corpus examples such as `整緊布甸`.
3. **`緊` and `住` are not interchangeable.** Sources contrast dynamic progressive `V緊` with continuous or maintained-state `V住`, including differences between main and subordinate/accompanying predicates.
4. **Lexical and event compatibility cannot be defined by the current closed `action_verb` list.** Motion, state-like, and extended-process `V緊` examples are attested, while aspect selection remains restricted.
5. **The progressive VP may occur inside a larger clause or discourse relation.** This does not create a special progressive-clause construction.
6. **`有 + V緊` is disputed and highly restricted, not categorically absent.** The literature reports disagreement and a few low-frequency or emphatic examples. The candidate therefore excludes this environment without declaring it ungrammatical.
7. **A cited future/modal `會 + V緊` context is rejected.** This boundary is recorded for the packet, not generalized to every modal or future context.
8. **`緊`, `住`, `過`, `咗`, and `吓` remain distinct overt aspect markers.** No one marker licenses another marker's semantics or distribution.

## Exact proposed subtype scope

All of the following would be required:

- an overt lexical predicate;
- immediately postverbal `緊`;
- an overt non-interrogative object or NP;
- a progressive interpretation supported by the predicate and construction;
- transparent preservation of predicate, `緊`, and object spans.

The subtype would not require an overt subject or `而家`, and it would not classify the subject as agent when the source does not establish that role.

## Explicit non-targets

- preverbal `喺度 + VP`;
- postverbal `住`, `過`, `咗`, or `吓`;
- objectless `V緊`;
- state-like or motion examples without an overt object;
- wh-object progressives such as `食緊咩`;
- progressive location questions;
- directional-progressive composition;
- `有 + V緊`;
- modal/future `會 + V緊`;
- negation, A-not-A, omitted arguments, or hidden reference times;
- a free `verb × 緊 × object` cross-product;
- a claim that the runtime `action_verb` inventory is a linguistic compatibility model.

## Competing analyses retained

- suffix, particle, and marker terminology for `緊`;
- lexical-aspect, event-structure, and selectional accounts of compatibility;
- simple-clause progressive versus discourse-backgrounding functions;
- whether broad `ProgressiveVP` should remain a public alias, become a review-only umbrella, or be decomposed into aspect plus argument structure;
- whether `ProgressiveTransitivePredicate` should merge into the subtype or retire as an internal duplicate.

No theory is selected beyond the overt order and narrow progressive interpretation.

## Implementation blockers

1. The current `action_verb` gate is neither demonstrably exhaustive nor a source-backed compatibility model.
2. Current object recognition misses several source-attested nouns, modified NPs, and coordinated objects.
3. The broad objectless `ProgressiveVP` currently appears in disputed or source-negative environments, including tested `有 + V緊` and future/modal strings.
4. Existing full-root coverage can pass while the progressive object span is wrong.
5. No fresh prospective evaluator custody has been selected and sealed for this package.
6. Native review has not yet tested a controlled lexical and structural contrast set.

## Freeze decision

The authority-origin question, source propositions, target, non-targets, and visible research packet are frozen as `CP023-P1-PROG01-R1`. This is a research checkpoint only. It authorizes no runtime, label-status, lexicon, Jyutping, role, fixture-expectation, or grammar change.
