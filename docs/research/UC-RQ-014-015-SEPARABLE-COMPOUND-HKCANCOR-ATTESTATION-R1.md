# UC-RQ-014/015 — HKCanCor separable-compound attestation

## Scope and method

This checkpoint uses the official CC BY UTF-8 HKCanCor release at
`fcbond/hkcancor` commit `39aeadf920e0b5ca93d0ad7792c59e740e7bdd65`
(archive SHA-256
`09223963b8756254e15353cad843f8a4b0cbc4b9223dc8a8fa27fb1cf846057e`).

The search was deliberately item-level. It checked the source-backed VO item
`結婚 git3fan1` and the published non-VO exemplars `影印 jing2jan3`,
`同居 tung4geoi1`, `地震 dei6zan3`, `小息 siu2sik1`, `拍拖 paak3to1`,
`比賽 bei2coi3`, `散步 saan3bou6`, `遲到 ci4dou3`, `相睇 soeng1tai2`,
and `大肚 daai6tou5`. Exact contiguous tokens and token sequences split by the
paper's tested short interveners were searched. This is an attestation check, not
a complete compound census or a grammaticality experiment.

## UC-RQ-014 VO item profile

HKCanCor contains sixteen contiguous `結婚/v/git3fan1/` tokens and four clear
separated realizations:

| Corpus locator | Surface | Adjudication |
|---|---|---|
| `FC-107_v2:1403–1405` | `佢啲同事結咗婚未啊？` | VO compound split by perfective `咗` |
| `FC-107_v2:1427–1429` | `有啲結咗婚𡃉喇好似。` | second natural perfective token |
| `FC-107_v2:1611–1613` | `會唔會結完婚一年都唔夠就有仔……？` | split by completive/resultative `完` |
| `FC-044_v2:6640–6642` | `……四十幾歲結晒婚生晒仔……` | split by completive/distributive `晒` |

The coexistence of the contiguous lexical token and three distinct separators is
positive natural evidence for an item-specific discontinuous `結婚` profile. It
does not license those separators for other VO compounds or establish that every
surface `結 + material + 婚` retains the stored compound meaning.

## UC-RQ-015 non-VO item check

The focused corpus search found only contiguous tokens for the attested published
items: `影印` three times, `小息` four times, `拍拖` once, `遲到` three times,
and `比賽` in nominal, verbal, and verbal-nominal uses. For example,
`FC-020_v:9576` has contiguous `影印` followed by perfective `咗`:
`你影印咗……一千個 copy 啊`. `幫襯`, an inseparable contrast in the source,
also occurs only contiguously in seven exact tokens.

No exact token was found for `同居`, `地震`, `散步`, `相睇`, or `大肚`, and no
tested short-separator sequence was found between the components of the checked
non-VO items. These absences do not overturn Chan and Cheung's web, judgment, and
experimental evidence. HKCanCor is a small 1997–1998 conversational corpus and
does not supply the contextualized natural tokens needed to promote any one of
these non-VO separator profiles.

## Disposition

| Action | Result |
|---|---|
| **Promote** | UC-RQ-014 gains a naturally attested typed lexical profile for `結婚`, with contiguous realization and separation by `咗`, `完`, and `晒`. |
| **Quarantine** | Extrapolation from `結婚` to the 878-item VO inventory, additional separators, movement, and automatic splitting remains unsupported. |
| **Promote** | UC-RQ-015 remains a source-established non-VO separable-compound research family, including its final-component-movement prohibition. |
| **Quarantine** | Natural item-by-separator licenses for the checked non-VO exemplars remain unconfirmed by this corpus and require contextualized audio or speaker evidence. |
| **Merge** | UC-RQ-014 and UC-RQ-015 should share a future lexical-identity ledger and compose with independently supported aspect/result structure; their VO and non-VO licenses must remain typed and separate. |
| **Retire** | Retire unrestricted “VO compounds are separable,” generic non-VO splitting, final-component movement for non-VO items, and inference of separation from an unsplit compound followed by aspect. |

No parser, lexicon, schema, or grammar-status change follows from this packet.
