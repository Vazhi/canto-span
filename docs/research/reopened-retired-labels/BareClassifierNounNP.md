# BareClassifierNounNP

**State:** `research_pending`; no runtime authorization.  
**Historical input:** retired `BareClassifierNP`.

## Narrow question

Does Cantonese license an overt `CLASSIFIER + NOUN` nominal without a numeral as a distinct constrained NP subtype?

## Why reopened

The old label was ambiguous because it could imply classifier-alone ellipsis. Peer-reviewed Cantonese classifier research instead documents classifier+noun sequences without numerals, including definite readings and distributional restrictions.

## Existing evidence to reverify

- `SRC-BOND-SIO-2024-CLASSIFIERS`
- `SRC-CHENG-SYBESMA-1999-NP`
- `SRC-SIMPSON-SOH-NOMOTO-2011-BARE-CL`

## Required boundaries

Test definite versus indefinite readings, syntactic position, information structure, classifier–noun compatibility, and regional variation. Do not insert a hidden numeral or noun, and do not license classifier-alone forms.

## Implementation safeguard

The retired matcher remains retired. Any future implementation belongs in the shared NP subsystem and requires source-linked positives and absence boundaries.
