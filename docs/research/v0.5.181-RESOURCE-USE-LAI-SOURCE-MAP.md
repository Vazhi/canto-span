## Target representation

Internal label: `ResourceUseLaiFunctionRelation`

Public compatibility label: `IntendedFunctionRelation`

The accepted subtype is deliberately narrower than the broad public relation. It represents only an overt non-person resource, artifact, or spatial part followed directly by `用嚟` or `用來` and an overt non-perfective action predicate stating assigned or intended function.

## Natural-attestation evidence

### Wong 2023

The inspected language sample contains the exact sentence `呢個用嚟切嘢`. It establishes the direct resource-function surface and also makes an instrumental/serial-verb analysis a relevant competitor. It does not determine whether the resource is a grammatical subject or topic.

### Hong Kong Memory oral-history narrative

The interview-derived page describes an entrance structure and states `另一邊用來儲物`. This independently attests an overt spatial resource plus `用來` plus an overt storage-function predicate. Because the page is editorialized narrative rather than marked verbatim speech, it is limited to this exact spatial-resource profile.

### Countryside Conservation Office direct quotation

An official government project page directly quotes a village homeowner: `呢間屋用嚟擺嘢、養豬養牛`. This independently attests an overt house resource plus adjacent `用嚟` and overt coordinated function predicates. One speaker and context do not establish unrestricted lexical or coordination productivity.

## Structural support

Cheung 2018 describes purposive `V1 + 嚟 + V2` and notes that `用來/用嚟` has become a conventionalized disyllabic expression. It supports the linker analysis but is not counted as a fourth natural attestation.

## Boundary and competing-analysis evidence

Chor 2018 supplies broader purposive, participant, and actual-use profiles. A separate overt user, perfective event, or broader purpose chain is outside the accepted subtype.

Matthews 2006 documents restrictions and indeterminate classifications in serial-purpose constructions. Surface adjacency alone cannot license the subtype.

## Required positive scope

1. overt non-person resource, artifact, or spatial part;
2. adjacent `用` plus `嚟` or `來`;
3. overt non-perfective action predicate expressing assigned or intended function;
4. no separate overt user, modal, copula, negator, perfective actual-use material, or hidden argument.

## Explicit exclusions

The subtype does not license:

- separate overt users;
- modal affordance or copular readings;
- perfective or actual-use events;
- ordinary instrumental `用`;
- general purposive `嚟` without the direct profile;
- negated `用嚟`;
- A-not-A or reduplicated forms;
- missing resources or predicates;
- person-like resources;
- hidden subject, object, noun, resource, purpose, result, or activity insertion.

## Representation safeguards

- Resource subject/topic status remains underdetermined.
- Elliptical `呢個` remains overt and receives no hidden noun.
- The public label remains `IntendedFunctionRelation` for compatibility.
- Broad `IntendedFunctionRelation` remains provisional.
- Vocabulary and Jyutping repairs do not provide grammar evidence.
