## Strongest round-one findings

1. `V完咗O` is directly attested and structurally analyzed; the unresolved issue is lexical, discourse, speaker, and regional distribution.
2. Purposive `嚟` is a directly supported Cantonese grammaticalized use.
3. `Agent V咗 NP 嚟 VP` belongs to a directly supported purpose-serial family, but VP1 lexical classes remain unresolved.
4. `Resource NP + Agent + 用嚟 VP` may be ordinary Cantonese hanging-topic/double-subject syntax combined with a use-function predicate; dedicated construction status is not established.
5. `用咗嚟 VP` is structurally plausible composition of perfective actual use plus purposive `嚟`, but exact corpus distribution remains missing.
6. Postobject locatives divide event location from result/goal location and require verb-class-sensitive analysis.
7. `V咗喇` combines verbal perfective and clause-level change-of-state/current-relevance meanings; it is not defined by object omission.
8. `用嚟做咩㗎` and coordinated-purpose strings are best treated compositionally unless later work finds construction-specific restrictions.
