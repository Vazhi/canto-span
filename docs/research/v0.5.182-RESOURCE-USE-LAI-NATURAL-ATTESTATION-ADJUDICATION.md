## Exact acceptance scope

All conditions are required:

1. an overt non-person resource, artifact, or spatial part;
2. adjacent `用` plus `嚟` or `來`;
3. an overt non-perfective action predicate stating the resource's assigned or intended function;
4. no separate overt user, modal, copula, negator, perfective actual-use event, or hidden argument.

The resource's subject/topic status remains underdetermined.
