## Instrument classification

The instrument is frozen as `pilot_binary_fixed_order_context_free_global_comment`.

Its limitations are material:

1. The binary scale collapses fully natural and merely acceptable responses.
2. Items appeared in a fixed order and were heavily concentrated on one visible construction family.
3. There were no unrelated filler/calibration items.
4. Ellipsis and interpretation-sensitive examples were presented without context.
5. Several negative items confounded semantic plausibility with syntax.
6. A single comment box appeared after all sentence judgments, making sentence-specific corrections difficult.
7. Naturalness alone cannot reveal which structural analysis licensed an acceptable string.

For these reasons, the 23 responses are counted as panel evidence but `promotion_eligible_independent_speaker_count` remains 0.

## Results

### Strong support for ordinary direct-resource surfaces

- `呢把刀用嚟切肉。`: 21/23 natural or acceptable (91.3%).
- `呢個用嚟切嘢。`: 22/23 (95.7%).
- `呢個杯用嚟飲水。`: 21/23 (91.3%).
- `呢張枱用嚟食飯。`: 21/23 (91.3%).
- `呢支筆用嚟寫字。`: 19/23 (82.6%).

This supports the ordinary surface profile `resource/instrument NP + 用嚟 + purpose/action VP` for this predominantly Hong Kong panel. It does not establish the parser's full resource class, exhaustive predicate scope, or a unique constituent analysis.

### Accepted broader frames that must not be folded into the narrow subtype

- `呢個可以用嚟切嘢。`: 20/23 (87.0%).
- `啲錢我用嚟買晒嗰堆嘢。`: 20/23 (87.0%).
- `呢個係用嚟切嘢。`: 23/23 (100%).
- `我買刀嚟切嘢。`: 18/23 (78.3%).
- `呢個用咗嚟切嘢。`: 19/23 (82.6%).
- `我用嚟切嘢。`: 20/23 (87.0%).

These strings are acceptable to most respondents, but they instantiate or permit modal, overt-user/topic, copular, general purposive-chain, actual-use, or ellipsis/omitted-object analyses. Their acceptability is evidence against treating every acceptable `用...嚟` string as the narrow direct-resource subtype.

### Useful boundary evidence

- `呢個用嚟切咗嘢。`: 3/23 natural or acceptable (13.0%).
- `呢個用嚟。`: 4/23 (17.4%) in isolation.
- `呢個唔用嚟切嘢。`: 7/23 (30.4%).
- `呢個用唔用嚟切嘢？`: 7/23 (30.4%).

The `用嚟 + V咗` result is the cleanest narrow boundary in this set. The no-predicate result is limited to isolation because no licensing context was supplied. Negation and A-not-A remain boundary candidates rather than categorical exclusions until better matched contextual contrasts are collected.

### Confounded items

- `香港用嚟切嘢。`: 1/23 (4.3%).
- `呢個問題用嚟切嘢。`: 0/23.

These combine structural manipulation with severe semantic implausibility, so rejection cannot be assigned uniquely to syntax.

- `呢張紙用來寫字。`: 12/23 (52.2%).
- `用來用去。`: 15/23 (65.2%).

These combine construction judgments with written-form/register reactions to `來`; one respondent explicitly commented that `來` is not Cantonese.

### Control warning

`我用刀切嘢。` received 17/23 natural or acceptable responses (73.9%), but 6/23 rejected it. The majority result is compatible with ordinary Cantonese, while the rejection rate confirms that this pilot did not perfectly isolate grammatical acceptability from survey strategy, context, or stylistic preference.
