# Stage A — Blind Native-Speaker Review

Please review the sentences as a speaker of Hong Kong Cantonese. Work item by item without consulting the project, parser output, translations, source notes, or other reviewers.

For every item:

1. Choose exactly one `naturalness_choice`: `NATURAL_IN_CONTEXT`, `UNNATURAL_IN_CONTEXT`, `UNSURE_OR_NEEDS_CONTEXT`, or `NOT_SEEN`.
2. Give your own translation or paraphrase. If there is more than one possible reading, list each one.
3. Describe who does what, what changes hands or is made, and who receives, benefits, permits, or undergoes anything—using your own words. Do not force a technical label.
4. If context is needed, say so and provide a minimal natural context.
5. If the sentence is marked or unacceptable, offer a preferred form if possible.
6. Comment on `畀` versus `俾` only if spelling affects your judgment.
7. Record confidence from 1 (low) to 5 (high), reviewer background, and date.

Do not leave a response blank. Use `UNSURE_OR_NEEDS_CONTEXT` or `NOT_SEEN` where appropriate. Your response is evidence about your judgment in this task, not a vote on a parser rule.
