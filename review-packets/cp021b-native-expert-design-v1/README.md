# CP021B Native / Expert Design Review Packet v1

Status: `PREPARED_NOT_STARTED`

This packet collects the external judgments required before either CP021B future design candidate may advance. It does not authorize parser design or runtime work.

## Two locked stages

1. Give only `stage-a-native/` to each native Hong Kong Cantonese reviewer. Do not provide project claims, intended translations, expected observations, source classes, parser output, or the expert form before their response is locked.
2. Preserve each completed Stage A form unchanged and record its SHA-256.
3. Give the locked Stage A responses plus `stage-b-expert/` and the cited CP021B evidence ledgers to the expert reviewer.
4. Preserve disagreement. Do not convert blanks, uncertainty, or “needs context” into acceptance or rejection.

The twelve items are a frozen, hash-ordered subset of the CP021B design partition: seven lexical-GIVE/order items relevant to `CP021B-PI-C01` and five post-theme items relevant to `CP021B-PI-C02`. No held-out row appears in this packet.

## What this packet cannot establish

- A natural surface judgment does not validate a parser hierarchy, category, participant role, or productivity claim.
- One reviewer or one task does not establish population frequency or unrestricted productivity.
- Expert structural analysis must retain theory and confidence boundaries.
- Generated probes remain probes, not independent language evidence.
- Review results cannot accept CP020R2 or bypass its rendered Obsidian review.

## Response vocabulary

Stage A `naturalness_choice` must be one of:

- `NATURAL_IN_CONTEXT`
- `UNNATURAL_IN_CONTEXT`
- `UNSURE_OR_NEEDS_CONTEXT`
- `NOT_SEEN`

Stage B controlled fields and their allowed values are defined in `stage-b-expert/README.md`.

Replace `UNFILLED` only with a reviewer-supplied response. Add reviewers as separate completed files; do not merge or average their responses in the raw packet.
